Zimbabwe Land Commission!

ROUTINE FARM INSPECTIONS

SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES

  1. To assess the levels of productivity, infrastructure development, mechanisation and environmental sustainability.
  2. To identify land use with regards to enterprise, ecological region and size of holding.
  3. To assess access to markets and financial resources.
  4. To identify challenges faced by farmers in realising income growth and improved standards of living.
  5. To assess the extent of gender mainstreaming in land allocations.
  6. To identify tenure documents possessed by the various farmer categories and the land rights thereof.

CATEGORIES OF FARM INSPECTIONS

  1. Comprehensive Routine Farm Inspections
  2. Assessments for 99-Year Leases Applications
  3. Inspections for delisting of gazzetted indigenous and BIPPA farms.
